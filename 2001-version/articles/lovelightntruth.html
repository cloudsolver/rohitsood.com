Light Love and Truth
 

Through Life's maze of
Closed doors
Confused faces
Plastic smiles


Furtive glances
Electric fences
Rigid stances
Suppressed emotions


Conflicting desires
Exacting responsibilities
Spent Energies and
Misdirected Affections

Lets walk the path 
of light, love and truth.



-Rohit Sood, Feb 2000
