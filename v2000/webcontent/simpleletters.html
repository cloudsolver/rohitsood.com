Simple Letters
 

Sometimes when one sits to write a formal letter, the tendency to use complicated words for a simple objective is the greatest headache for the recipient of the epistle. Keep your corporate letters simple, try to get ideas across, rather than words.

So keep this list in mind when you write the next letter. (I will be adding other common words shortly)

Use This Not This
A day per diem
Essential  sine qua non
End  terminate
Do away with obviate

Change modification

As such  per se
Aware  cognizant
Advice  input
About  approximately
A year  per annum
